Description

This solution was created to wrap any game and run jackpots on top of it, without the games having to be adapted. This was mostly to drive traffick outside of peak gameplay times and push other game genres into the jackpot exclusive arena.

My Contribution

I was picked as part of a task team, to develop a solution within 6 weeks, that could wrap any game and turn it into a jackpots game. From there I became the lone product owner I managed everything, from sourcing business requirements, to dev and design support.
